Why Now · Why Belize

The Last Affordable
Caribbean Frontier

San Pedro is the #1 tourist destination in Belize — and real estate prices are still a fraction of what you'd pay in Cancun, Costa Rica, or the Caymans. Buyers who moved early on comparable Caribbean markets have seen significant long-term returns.

I

USD Economy

Belize is fully dollarized. No currency risk, no conversion headaches. What you see is what you pay.

II

World-Class Tourism

The Great Blue Hole, the Belize Barrier Reef, world-class diving. Rental demand from US travelers is year-round and growing.

III

Rising Property Values

San Pedro has seen consistent appreciation as infrastructure improves and expat demand increases. Early buyers win.

IV

Direct Flights from US

Direct routes from Houston, Dallas, Miami, Atlanta and more. Under 3 hours from most of the US South and East Coast.

V

Foreigner-Friendly Laws

US citizens can own freehold property with the same rights as Belizean citizens. No restrictions, no red tape.

VI

Strong Rental Yields

Well-managed short-term rentals on the island routinely generate 8–14% gross yields. Your property can pay for itself.
